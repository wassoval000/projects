The Competition

This Year the competitions for both VEX and FIRST robotics have a theme. They are based upon outer space due to the face that the anniversary of the moon landing is coming up.

The United States' Apollo 11 manned moon mission landed on the moon September 13, 1959. This makes 2019 the year of its 50th anniversary.

To this day, the United States is the only country to conduct manned missions to the Moon successfully, with us last departing the surface of the moon December 1972.
